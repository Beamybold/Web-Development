Nigerian Communication Commission

NCC is the Nigerian Communications Commission, the independent regulatory authority for Nigeria's telecommunications industry.It was established in 1992 under Decree 75 and later formalized by the Nigerian Communications Act (NCA) 2003.

About Us

The primary responsibilities of NCC are:

  • ensuring fair competition among operators
  • providing qualitative telecommunications services
  • protecting consumer interests
  • setting industry standards
  • promoting ICT use for national development
