Animation Best Practices

  • Performance: Animate transform and opacity for best performance
  • Duration: Keep animations short (0.2s - 0.5s for interactions)
  • Purpose: Use animations to enhance UX, not distract
  • Accessibility: Respect prefers-reduced-motion setting
